Models in the Case Study for the Dynamic Evolution of Context-Aware Systems

The source files of the models in the case study for the dynamic evolution of context-aware systems are as follows (see the figure below). You can open them on a text editor or with the indicated tools.

  • Requirements Model (open with OpenOME)
  • Architecture Model (open with Papyrus using a SoaML plug-in)
  • Variability Model (open with MOSkitt4SPL)
  • Tactic Model (open with MOSkitt4SPL)
  • Context Model (open with Protégé)
