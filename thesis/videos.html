Video Demonstration of the Dynamic Adaptation of Service Compositions

 

In this demonstration, our Model-based Reconfiguration Engine for Web Services (MoRE-WS) uses models at runtime to guide two dynamic adaptations in our case study. MoRE-WS implements the Model-Based Reconfigurator. In order to watch this video on HD, please open it directly on Vimeo.

 

 

This video was introduced in the following journal article:

 

Alférez, G.H., Pelechano, V., Mazo, R., Salinesi, C., Diaz, D., 2013. Dynamic adaptation of service compositions with variability models. Journal of Systems and Software. URL
